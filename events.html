Children's Day!

The first Saturday of every month we have a special event, each one with a different theme. This month's Saturday we have an event titled, "Children's Day". This event will start at 12:00 PM and will end at 6:00. We will be showing our feature children's film, "The Nightmare Before Christmas". Children will be free during this event and adults will have a discount of $5:00, we will also have free refreshments and dicounted food prices. Anyone is welcome and for those who cannot make it, this film will be showing throughout the whole month of October.

The Nightmare Before Christmas

The Pumpkin King of Halloween Town, Jack Skellington, stumbles upon a new place called Christmas Town. After years of only celebrating Halloween, he decides things are going to be different this year.

Showtimes: (1:00 PM - 2:20 PM) (2:30 PM - 3:50 PM) (4:00 PM - 5:20 PM)
