About Us

AVON Cinemas is a Providence born, independent theatre that loves to play classic films as well as independent films to help get new directors noticed. We have been around since 1938 and have not left our roots by sticking to our Art Deco theme. AVON Cinemas has a special place in the city of Providence's heart as well as everyone who visits us.
